Important Note

Boilerplate removal corpus par depend karta hai:

Agar data plain .txt files hai, to fixed rules ya regex kaafi hote hain.
Agar data websites se crawl kiya gaya hai, to BeautifulSoup, Readability, ya Trafilatura jaise tools use kiye jaate hain.
Large-scale web corpora (jaise Common Crawl) me boilerplate removal ek dedicated preprocessing step hota hai jo main content ko navigation aur repeated page elements se alag karta hai.
